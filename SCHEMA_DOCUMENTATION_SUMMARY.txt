## 🚀 For Your "Default Fleet" Feature

**Directly addresses your use case:**

**Query to assess impact** (in DatabaseSchema-QuickRef.md):
```sql
SELECT sr.SeriesId, COUNT(DISTINCT r.FleetId) AS FleetCount
FROM SeriesRace sr
INNER JOIN Race r ON sr.RaceId = r.Id
GROUP BY sr.SeriesId
HAVING COUNT(DISTINCT r.FleetId) > 1
```

**Important caveat** (highlighted in docs):
> A Series can contain races from different Fleets (this is possible and valid)

This means:
- ⚠️ Can't simply add a `DefaultFleetId` to Series for all cases
- ⚠️ Multi-fleet series need special handling
- ✅ Query above helps identify problematic series
- ✅ Docs explain the constraint clearly
