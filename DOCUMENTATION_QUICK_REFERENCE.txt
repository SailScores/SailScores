## 💡 For Your "Default Fleet" Feature

### Key Constraints to Remember

1. **Multi-Fleet Series Exist**
   - A series CAN contain races from different fleets
   - Query to find them is provided
   - Cannot simply add one DefaultFleetId to Series table

2. **Detection Query**
   ```sql
   SELECT sr.SeriesId, COUNT(DISTINCT r.FleetId) AS FleetCount
   FROM SeriesRace sr
   INNER JOIN Race r ON sr.RaceId = r.Id
   GROUP BY sr.SeriesId
   HAVING COUNT(DISTINCT r.FleetId) > 1
   ```

3. **Impact Assessment**
   - Run this query to see how many series are affected
   - Multi-fleet series need special handling
   - May affect your feature design
